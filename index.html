Chief AI Officer at GoodHelp.AI
Unlocking Entrepreneurship

GoodHelp.AI

GoodHelp.ai aims to unlock entrepreneurship with AI: so much of running a business is the stuff that you aren't exciting about doing-- taxes, accounting, marketing, and so on. At GoodHelp.AI, we're building AI that helps SMB owners focus on what they love by taking the rest off their plate at a fraction of the cost that a professional services firm would charge.

We're starting with content marketing: GoodHelp.AI will create a comprehensive marketing plan for your company, develop a keyword strategy, and write high-quality, researched content to help you get in front of your customers.

Technical Advisor to Kevin Scott, CTO of Microsoft
OpenAI & AI Futures

Microsoft & OpenAI

As the Technical Advisor to Microsoft's CTO, Kevin Scott, Karmel oversaw Microsoft's technical partnership with OpenAI from its early, awkward phase through the launch of GPT-3.5, GPT-4, Copilot, ChatGPT, Azure OpenAI Platform and more.

Additionally, Karmel worked across both companies, Microsoft Research, and startups to identify new trends in AI and help Microsoft position itself and its products for the AI age.

Towards a Science of Consciousness
Writing & Speaking

Understanding Consciousness

AI teaches us something fundamental about consciousness, and it points to a science of consciousness that could change how we experience being human. Just as an AI can think by computing over digital state, human thought and consciousness can be understood as computation over state stored in matter.

Bringing together philosophy and computer science, Karmel writes and speaks on the ways in which AI will change the way we understand ourselves.
